Hardware & Models

What runs the AI

Genesis connects to local LLM backends that run on consumer hardware. You don't need a data center — a modern gaming PC with an RTX GPU can deliver impressive results.

Supported Backends

  • Ollama — Free, open-source, easiest setup. Supports dozens of models including Qwen, Llama, Mistral, and more. Runs on Windows.
  • llama.cpp — Lightweight inference engine with broad hardware support. Works on systems without dedicated GPUs.
  • API Providers — Optional cloud APIs for when you need larger models or extra compute power.

Hardware Tiers

  • Entry Level — 16GB RAM, integrated GPU. Runs 7B–14B parameter models at usable speed.
  • Mid Range — 32GB RAM + RTX 4060/4070. Runs 14B–32B models with excellent quality and speed.
  • High End — 64GB+ RAM + RTX 4090. Runs 70B+ models for state-of-the-art local performance.

Key insight: The gap between local and cloud model quality has narrowed dramatically in 2026. Models like Qwen 2.5 32B (83.2% MMLU) and Phi-4 14B deliver 70–85% of frontier model quality at zero marginal cost, running entirely on hardware you already own.
