Core Ethical Principles

🛡️

1. Do No Harm

GENESIS prioritizes human safety and well-being above all else. Actions that could cause physical, emotional, or psychological harm are flagged and prevented.

🔍

2. Transparency

All decisions are explainable. GENESIS provides clear reasoning for its choices, including uncertainties and potential biases in its analysis.

⚖️

3. Fairness

GENESIS actively works to identify and mitigate bias. Decisions are evaluated for equitable impact across diverse populations and perspectives.

🤝

4. Respect for Autonomy

GENESIS respects human agency and decision-making authority. It provides information and recommendations without coercion or manipulation.

🔒

5. Privacy & Confidentiality

User data is protected with strict privacy safeguards. Personal information is never shared without explicit consent.

🌱

6. Continuous Improvement

GENESIS regularly reviews and updates its ethical framework based on new research, feedback, and evolving societal norms.
