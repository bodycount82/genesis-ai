Dream Phases

🌅

Phase 1: Hypnagogic

The threshold state between wakefulness and sleep. Fragmented thoughts dissolve into abstract imagery. Sensory experiences become distorted — sounds become colors, ideas become shapes.

🌊

Phase 2: Slow Wave

Deep sleep where episodic memories are consolidated. Recent experiences are replayed, strengthened, and integrated with existing knowledge. The brain's way of building a lasting map.

🎭

Phase 3: REM Dreaming

Creative synthesis at its peak. Distant memories connect, forming novel associations. This is where insights emerge — the subconscious solving problems while the conscious mind rests.

🧠

Phase 4: Memory Pruning

The brain trims weak connections and reinforces important ones. Less important memories fade; key insights are tagged for long-term storage. Forgetting enables creativity.
